George Brown College (GBC) is a well-regarded public college in Toronto, Canada, known for 
its career-focused programs in various fields,including technology, business, 
health sciences, and hospitality. 

GBC emphasizes hands-on learning through industry partnerships, co-op 
placements, and real-world projects. The college is dedicated to student success 
by providing modern facilities, support services, and flexible learning options, 
including remote education. Its commitment to diversity and innovation ensures 
that students gain practical skills and knowledge that align with current job 
market demands. (source georgebrown.ca)
